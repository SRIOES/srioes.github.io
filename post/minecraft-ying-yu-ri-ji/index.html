Minecraft英语日记

I’m going to write about the computer game “Minecraft” again. 

I have two reasons: First, the article I wrote was terrible. Second, Minecraft is such a really good game that it’s worth writing an extra (or reloaded) article for it.
Firstly, the survival system is very clever and good. The studio Mojang (who created Minecraft) made their best to do the survival system and it’s very educational to try about it.
Secondly, there are a lot of mods that created by players in Minecraft. This point is also the most famous and important one. The mods now can include most things that you can imagine such as industries, foods, galaxy, and even magic. These things not only can improve your gaming experience, but also can make you learn the knowledge about them while you are playing. So I really like this game very much because I can always learn something that can’t be got by reading from it. For example, I learn how to survival and how to build industry system. Also, I really enjoy this time of playing and studying.
To be honest, computer games always make people want to play them all the time. But Minecraft doesn’t do this. You can stop playing it everytime.
What such a good game!
